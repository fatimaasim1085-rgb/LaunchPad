About LaunchPad

A conceptual venture built by NUST students to solve a real problem in Pakistan's digital economy.

Our Story

The Problem We're Solving

Pakistan has the 3rd largest freelancing workforce in the world — yet 72% of new freelancers quit within their first month. Not because they lack skills, but because the existing platforms were never built for them.

Fiverr and Upwork are global marketplaces designed for established professionals. They offer no mentorship, no local payment options, no guidance — and a competitive environment that immediately overwhelms someone who's just starting out.

LaunchPad is our answer to that gap. A platform built from scratch for first-time freelancers in Pakistan — with a structured path from zero to earning, a mentor system that pairs you with experienced professionals, and payments via EasyPaisa and JazzCash so there's no PayPal barrier.

This is a conceptual venture developed as part of our Entrepreneurship Minor at NUST School of Health Sciences. Every part of the platform — from the tech architecture to the funding strategy — is documented and designed to be buildable.

Accessibility First

Local language, local payments, mobile-first. Designed for students in Gujrat, not just professionals in Karachi.

Mentorship as Infrastructure

The mentor system isn't a nice-to-have — it's the core mechanism that separates LaunchPad from every competitor.

Structured Progression

A two-tier system with clear criteria gives freelancers a visible goal to work toward, not an endless grind.

Real-World Impact

Every design decision maps back to SDGs 4, 5, 8, and 10 — employment, gender equity, quality education, reduced inequality.

Build Roadmap

From Concept to Launch

Now

Conceptual Blueprint Complete

Full product design, tech architecture, revenue model, and funding strategy documented. This website is the deliverable.

Phase 1

MVP Development ($5–10K)

Build core platform — user registration, skill quiz, basic gig listings, in-app chat, and EasyPaisa integration. Test with 50 beta users in Islamabad and Gujrat.

Phase 2

Angel Funding + Full Launch ($50K)

Mentor matching engine, AI Profile Assistant, escrow system, and mobile app. Nationwide launch with i2i Ventures angel backing.

Year 2

Scale + VC Round ($500K+)

Corporate accounts, skill certification system, Payoneer international integration, and Series A from Indus Valley Capital or Fatima Ventures.
